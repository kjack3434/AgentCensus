# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a welcoming,
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our standards

Behavior that helps a positive environment includes:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.
- Showing empathy toward other people.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment of any kind, personal or political attacks, publishing others'
private information without permission, and other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate
in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and when an individual is representing the project in
public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the project maintainers using the private
reporting channel described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly, and maintainers will respect the privacy and security of reporters.
